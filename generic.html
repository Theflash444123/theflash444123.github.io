My Journey

My journey began as a calibration engineer in the research and development wing of Bajaj Auto Limited. As part of my job, I was constantly working with large amounts of data generated from the calibration process, and it got me thinking about how to handle this data in a more efficient manner.

That's when I decided to explore the world of programming, specifically Python, to simplify and automate some of the day-to-day activities that I was involved in. I started learning Python on my own and soon discovered that it was a powerful tool for data analysis and automation.

As I delved deeper into Python, I began to develop a Python library for calibration, which helped streamline the calibration process and made it more efficient. I continued to refine and improve the library, and eventually, it became a valuable asset for my team.

But my work didn't stop there. I wanted to take things to the next level and create an application that would help others generate insightful reports from the data generated during the calibration process. I spent countless hours developing the application and testing it, and finally, I was able to create a powerful tool that my colleagues could use to analyze and visualize data.

My success with Python and data analysis eventually led me to pursue a master's degree in data science, where I continued to hone my skills and gain a deeper understanding of the field. Today, I'm proud to say that I'm a successful data scientist, and I owe it all to that initial curiosity and drive to find a better way to handle the data generated during the calibration process.
